MN Beer Flow

Duration: 2-Week-Sprint

MN Beer Flow is a mobile-first web app designed to let users find, track, rate, and comment on their favorite Minnesota breweries. Users can use their current location to find the breweries that are closest to them, as well as find a random brewery within different distances from their location to visit.
